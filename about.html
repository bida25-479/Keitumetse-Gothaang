About BrightSight Kids

Background of our business

BrightSight Kids is a small business located in Tlokweng which focuses on selling eyeglass frames for childern. Our business started in 2020 with the idea of addressing the issue of growing need for eye glass frames providers in Botswana which are not only functional but stylish, comfortable and durable.
